    Nigel Dodds MP and the DUP

    In 2000, recognising the extent of persisting religious inequality in social housing in North Belfast, the SDLP made a public call for a housing strategy.
    Nigel Dodds, MP for north Belfast stated:

    “The reality is that ordinary Protestants on the ground have seen the area being run down and neglected over the years, having been forced out by the IRA and Sinn Fein Hitlerite Nazi tactics. What we are now seeing is demands led by nationalist politicians to take away the peace lines – it’s a recipe for civil war.”
