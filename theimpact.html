  • Equality Can't Wait campaign

    Equality Can't Wait Campaign

    Thanks to the work of families through the Equality Can't Wait campaign, the persisting religious inequality in housing provision, and the growing levels of homelessness in our society, has reamined on the agenda of decision makers.

    The United Nations Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights Committee, UN Rapporteur on the Right to Housing, Council of Europe Human Rights Commissioner, NI Human Rights Commission, Equality Commission, Children's Commissioner - and many others - have called on the inequality in north Belfast housing to be remedied.

    Despite breaching core legsilation in the Belfast Agreement, addressing this entrenched inequality has not been made a priority by any of the Executive's since the formation of Stormont in 1999.

  • Alternative Plans for Hillview!

    Alternative Plans for Hillview

    In 2017, members of the Equality Can't Wait campaign worked along with Barsav Architects to produce alternative plans for the Hillview site. In contrast to the retail-only development proposed by the developer, the Alternative Plan envisaged the building of a sustainable community.
